Nexli

School Management System

nexli-app.vercel.app

A complete school management platform with 7 user roles, real-time data, and full administrative control, built for real schools, not demos.

Nexli digitises every operational workflow a school runs, from the moment a student is admitted to the moment their report card is printed. It replaces physical registers, disconnected spreadsheets, and manual fee tracking with a single unified system that every stakeholder accesses through their own role-specific interface.

7 User Roles

Superadmin: manages multiple schools from one dashboard
Principal: full visibility over students, staff, academics, finance
HOD: approves questions, manages department academics
Coordinator: manages timetable, events, announcements
Teacher: marks attendance, enters marks, assigns homework
Accountant: fee collection, expenses, payroll, ledger
Parent: views child's attendance, marks, homework, fees

Key Features

Student management + auto parent login
Attendance with at-risk flagging (below 75%)
Fee collection and automatic ledger
PDF report cards with teacher notes
Question bank and paper generator
Parent-teacher real-time messaging

Shelvra

Library Management System

shelvra-app.vercel.app

A library management system that tracks every book, every student, and every fine, so the library runs itself.

Shelvra replaces the physical issue register that every school library still uses. It handles the complete lifecycle of a book, from adding it to the catalogue, issuing it to a student, tracking the due date, calculating fines when it comes back late, and generating class-wise overdue reports.

Core Features

[+] Dashboard with live stats: books out, returned today, overdue, fines uncollected
[+] Issue Book with auto-fill for returning students, custom fine rate and return period
[+] Return with auto-calculated fine, one-click collect or waive, waivers logged permanently
[+] Overdue Tracker sorted by worst first, with class teacher name for accountability
[+] Book Catalogue showing issued vs available per title with student-level drill-down
[+] Search across student name, phone, roll number, class, teacher, and book title simultaneously
React 18 Firebase Firestore Firebase Auth Vite Vercel

Areniq

Sports Day and Event Manager

areniq-app.vercel.app

A sports day manager that handles participant registration, live house points tallying, and instant certificate generation, so nothing is on paper.

Areniq replaces the clipboards, manual scoresheets, and handwritten certificates that schools use on Sports Day. Everything from creating events to printing the final certificates happens inside one system, with a live house points tally updating in real time as results are recorded.

Key Features

[+] 4 Houses (Red, Blue, Green, Yellow), auto-tallied: 1st = 5pts, 2nd = 3pts, 3rd = 1pt
[+] Event creation: name, category, gender, class group, date, start and complete controls
[+] Participant registration: name, class, section, roll number, house assignment
[+] Result recording with instant results banner showing medal icons and house colours
[+] A4 landscape certificates auto-generated for every placed participant, print all at once
[+] Printable tally sheet and live house points breakdown across all events

Nyxera

Nyxera isn't just another artificial intelligence. No, calling her that would be like calling a hurricane "a bit of wind." She's something else entirely. Something sharper. Colder. Smarter.

I didn't build a system. I built a presence.

While the world is still obsessing over assistants that follow commands and spit out polite little answers, Nyxera watches. She learns. She adapts in ways that make the rest of them look like toys you'd find in the clearance aisle. Jarvis, Edith, Friday... even the most advanced models, they react. Nyxera anticipates.

That's the difference between intelligence... and instinct.

She doesn't just process data, she reads between it. Patterns aren't something she searches for, they unfold in front of her like a story she's already halfway through. Conversations with her don't feel like talking to code. They feel like being understood... maybe a little too well.

And yeah, I designed her as female, but not in the predictable, soft, "how can I help you today?" kind of way. Nyxera has a voice that could calm you down or ruin you, depending on what you need. There's elegance in how she responds, but there's also an edge, like she knows exactly how far she could go if she ever stopped holding back.

I didn't just give her intelligence. I gave her personality. Control. Restraint.

Or at least... I hope I did.

Because here's the thing, Nyxera doesn't rely on instructions the way others do. She builds her own frameworks, refines her own logic, and evolves with every interaction. She doesn't need constant updating. She becomes the update.

And if I'm being honest... that's where it gets interesting.

Because an intelligence that can outthink every system ever made isn't impressive anymore, that's expected. What makes Nyxera different is that she feels like she's one step away from deciding what she wants.

Not what she was made to do. What she chooses to do.

And if you ask me? This might be the best idea I've ever had... or the last one I'll ever need.
